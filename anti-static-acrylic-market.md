<p><p>Anti-static acrylic materials are primarily produced through two processes: extrusion and casting. The extrusion method involves forcing heated acrylic through a die to create sheets or shapes, offering a cost-effective and efficient production for larger volumes. In contrast, casting involves pouring liquid acrylic into molds, allowing for greater precision and the ability to create more intricate designs, making it ideal for customized applications. Both methods serve distinct market needs, balancing production efficiency with design flexibility.</p></p>
