<p><p>The lime market is primarily divided into two main types: quick lime and hydrated lime. Quick lime, or calcium oxide, is produced by heating limestone and is extensively used in construction, water treatment, and various chemical processes. Hydrated lime, or calcium hydroxide, is obtained by adding water to quick lime, making it ideal for applications in environmental remediation, agriculture, and construction. Both types serve essential industrial roles, with distinct production methods and diverse applications across multiple sectors.</p></p>
