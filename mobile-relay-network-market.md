<p><p>The Mobile Relay Network market can be categorized into two primary types: Infrastructure Less and Infrastructure Based. Infrastructure Less networks operate without extensive physical setups, relying on existing mobile frameworks to facilitate communication among users. In contrast, Infrastructure Based markets involve dedicated installations of hardware and technology, such as base stations and antennas, to establish robust and reliable connectivity. Both types cater to different needs; Infrastructure Less networks offer flexibility and cost-effectiveness, while Infrastructure Based networks provide enhanced performance and coverage.</p></p>
