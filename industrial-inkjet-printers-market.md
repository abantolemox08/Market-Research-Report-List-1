<p><ul><li>CIJ</li><li>DOD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial inkjet printer market primarily comprises Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop-On-Demand (DOD) technologies. CIJ printers continuously circulate ink, creating a consistent stream of droplets, making them ideal for high-speed applications and various surfaces. In contrast, DOD printers release ink droplets only when needed, offering superior resolution and precision, suited for detailed graphics and specialized materials. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, including packaging, labeling, and manufacturing, enhancing productivity and product quality.</p></p>
