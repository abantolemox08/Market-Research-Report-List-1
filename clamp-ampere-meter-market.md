<p><p>Clamp ampere meters are essential tools for measuring current in electrical systems without disconnecting the circuit. Two main types are: </p><p>1. **Transformer Type**: These meters utilize a current transformer that clamps around a conductor, allowing for accurate current measurement based on magnetic induction.</p><p>2. **Electromagnetic System**: This type relies on electromagnetic principles, detecting the magnetic field generated by the current flowing through a conductor, providing real-time measurements. Both types are widely used in industrial and commercial applications for monitoring electrical performance.</p></p>
