<p><strong>The Alternator Decoupler Pulleys (ADP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overrunning Alternator Decoupler (OAD) Pulleys</li><li>One-Way Clutch (OWC) Pulleys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alternator Decoupler Pulleys (ADPs) enhance the efficiency of automotive alternators by allowing them to decouple from engine speed fluctuations. The market consists of two main types: Overrunning Alternator Decoupler (OAD) Pulleys, which enable the alternator to spin freely during deceleration, reducing engine load; and One-Way Clutch (OWC) Pulleys, which offer similar functionality but with different mechanical designs. Both types contribute to improved fuel efficiency, reduced vibration, and extended alternator life, catering to modern automotive needs.</p></p>
