<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) pumping systems for irrigation utilize solar energy to power water pumps, offering a sustainable solution for agricultural water management. These systems can be categorized into two main types: submersible and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are installed underwater and are ideal for accessing groundwater, providing efficient irrigation from deep wells. In contrast, surface pumps are positioned above the water source and are suited for shallow water bodies, facilitating irrigation in areas with accessible surface water.</p></p>
