<p><p>The weather forecasting equipment market comprises various instruments used to measure atmospheric conditions. Barometers gauge air pressure, while anemometers measure wind speed. Hygrometers assess humidity levels, and rain gauges collect precipitation data. Thermometers record temperature, and radiometers measure solar radiation. Scatterometers are utilized for wind vector observation over oceans, and spectrometers analyze light to identify atmospheric components. Collectively, these tools enable accurate weather predictions, supporting various sectors including agriculture, aviation, and disaster management.</p></p>
