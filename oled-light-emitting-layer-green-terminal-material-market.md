<p><strong>The OLED Light-emitting Layer Green Terminal Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Green Host Material</li><li>Green Dopant Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED light-emitting layer green terminal material market comprises two main types: green host materials and green dopant materials. Green host materials serve as the primary matrix that supports the light-emitting dopants, providing structural stability and enhancing light output. In contrast, green dopant materials are responsible for the actual emission of green light when stimulated by an electric current. Together, these components are crucial for achieving efficient, high-quality green emission in OLED displays and lighting applications, driving innovation in the industry.</p></p>
