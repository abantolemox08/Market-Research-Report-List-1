<p><p>The flight simulator market is categorized into three main types: Full Flight Simulators (FFS), Flight Training Devices (FTD), and others such as Flight Briefing Simulators (FBS) and Flight Management Simulators (FMS). FFS are advanced systems that replicate real flight environments with high fidelity, essential for pilot certification. FTD offer less immersive training, focusing on procedural practice. Other devices like FBS and FMS provide targeted training for specific skills, enhancing overall pilot competency while varying in complexity and cost.</p></p>
