<p><p>Silicon carbide paper is available in two primary market types: self-adhesive and non-adhesive. Self-adhesive silicon carbide paper features a sticky backing that allows for easy application on surfaces, making it ideal for detailed work and precise sanding tasks. In contrast, non-adhesive silicon carbide paper requires manual attachment to a sanding block or tool, providing flexibility for various sanding applications. Both types are widely used in automotive, woodworking, and metalworking industries for their superior cutting performance and durability.</p></p>
