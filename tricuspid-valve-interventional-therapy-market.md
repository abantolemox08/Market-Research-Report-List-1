<p><p>The Tricuspid Valve Interventional Therapy market includes various innovative techniques aimed at improving tricuspid valve function. Leaflet clamping focuses on reducing regurgitation by securing valve leaflets together. Annulus retraction involves altering the annular size to enhance valve coaptation and minimize backflow. Lastly, increasing the matching area aims to expand the contact surface between valve components, optimizing performance and reducing the incidence of annular dilation. Together, these methods offer effective solutions for tricuspid valve repair and treatment.</p></p>
