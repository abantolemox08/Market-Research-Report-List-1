<p><strong>The Methanol Fuel Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Methanol Fuel Cell</li><li>Indirect Methanol Fuel Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The methanol fuel cell market is primarily categorized into two types: Direct Methanol Fuel Cells (DMFC) and Indirect Methanol Fuel Cells (IMFC). DMFCs directly convert methanol into electricity through electrochemical reactions, offering high efficiency and ease of fuel handling, making them suitable for portable applications. In contrast, IMFCs involve a two-step process where methanol is first converted into hydrogen before generating electricity. This type typically offers higher energy density but may have more complex systems, making it suitable for stationary applications.</p></p>
