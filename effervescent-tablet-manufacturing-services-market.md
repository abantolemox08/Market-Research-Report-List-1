<p><ul><li>Wet Granulation</li><li>Dry Granulation</li><li>Direct Compression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Effervescent tablet manufacturing services involve distinct techniques tailored for producing effervescent formulations. Wet granulation combines liquid and powder to enhance particle size and flowability, suitable for moisture-sensitive ingredients. Dry granulation uses mechanical force to compact powders without liquids, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Direct compression involves compressing powdered ingredients directly into tablets, requiring excipients that facilitate binding without prior granulation. Each method addresses specific formulation needs, ensuring effective uniformity and stability in effervescent tablet production.</p></p>
