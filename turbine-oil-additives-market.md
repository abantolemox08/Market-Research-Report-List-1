<p><p>The Turbine Oil Additives market can be categorized into two main types: Single Component and Additive Package. Single Component additives are individual chemical substances designed to enhance specific properties of turbine oils, such as viscosity or oxidation stability. In contrast, Additive Packages are pre-formulated mixtures containing multiple additives that work synergistically to improve performance characteristics like anti-wear, corrosion protection, and thermal stability. These types cater to different formulation needs and performance requirements in turbine oil applications.</p></p>
