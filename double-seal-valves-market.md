<p><p>The double seal valves market is primarily categorized into single seat and double seat types. Single seat valves feature a single sealing area, making them compact and ideal for low-pressure applications, ensuring efficient flow control. In contrast, double seat valves incorporate two sealing surfaces, providing enhanced leakage prevention and better performance under higher pressures. This design allows for greater reliability in critical applications, making them suitable for various industries, including oil and gas, chemicals, and water treatment. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
