<p><p>The Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM) market is primarily divided into two types: manual and automatic systems. Manual ATGMs require operators to guide the missile towards the target, typically using vision systems and control mechanisms, offering precision but demanding high operator skill. In contrast, automatic ATGMs utilize advanced technologies such as infrared homing, laser guidance, or radar, enabling them to track and engage targets with minimal operator intervention. This automation increases responsiveness and effectiveness in combat scenarios, catering to diverse military needs.</p></p>
