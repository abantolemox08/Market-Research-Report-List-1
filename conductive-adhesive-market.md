<p><strong>The Conductive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic Conductive Adhesive (ICA).</li><li>Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive (ACA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: Isotropic Conductive Adhesive (ICA) and Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive (ACA). ICA allows electrical conductivity in all directions, making it suitable for applications such as surface mount technology and flexible circuits. ACA, on the other hand, offers conductivity along a specific direction, which is ideal for connecting components where reliability and precision are critical, such as in semiconductor packaging and displays. Both types cater to diverse electronic manufacturing needs.</p></p>
