<p><strong>The Thermal Measurement Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal measurement equipment is categorized into contact and non-contact types based on how temperature is assessed. </p><p>Contact thermal measurement involves direct physical interaction with the object, using devices like thermocouples and thermistors to ensure accurate readings. </p><p>In contrast, non-contact measurement uses infrared sensors or thermal imaging cameras to gauge temperature from a distance, making it ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing precision and convenience in various industries.</p></p>
