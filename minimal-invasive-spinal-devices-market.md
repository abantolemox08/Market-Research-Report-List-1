<p><strong>The Minimal Invasive Spinal Devices Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spine Fusion Procedure</li><li>Spine Non-fusion Procedure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The minimally invasive spinal devices market serves two primary applications: spine fusion and spine non-fusion procedures. Spine fusion procedures involve the surgical stabilization of vertebrae, often using devices like screws and rods to promote bone healing. In contrast, spine non-fusion procedures focus on alleviating pain and restoring function without permanently merging vertebrae, utilizing devices such as motion-preserving implants. Both approaches aim to minimize patient recovery time and improve surgical outcomes while addressing various spinal disorders.</p></p>
