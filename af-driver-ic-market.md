<p><ul><li>Open Loop Driver IC</li><li>Close Loop Driver IC</li><li>OIS Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AF Driver IC market consists of three main types: Open Loop Driver ICs, Close Loop Driver ICs, and OIS Driver ICs. Open Loop Driver ICs provide basic autofocus control without feedback, making them cost-effective but less precise. Close Loop Driver ICs are more advanced, using feedback mechanisms for improved accuracy in focus adjustment. OIS Driver ICs are specialized for Optical Image Stabilization, enhancing image clarity by compensating for camera shake. Each type caters to specific applications in imaging technology.</p></p>
