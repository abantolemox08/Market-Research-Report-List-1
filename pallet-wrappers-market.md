<p><p>Pallet wrappers are essential in logistics, designed to secure goods on pallets for transportation. The market is divided into two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic wrappers. Semi-automatic wrappers require manual intervention to load and start the wrapping process, offering flexibility and lower initial costs suitable for smaller operations. In contrast, fully automatic wrappers integrate advanced technology to autonomously handle the entire wrapping process, increasing efficiency and reducing labor costs, making them ideal for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
