<p><p>The Airport GPU market comprises two main types: mobile and fixed GPUs. Mobile GPUs are versatile ground power units that can be easily maneuvered to provide electrical and pneumatic power to aircraft at various locations, enhancing operational flexibility. In contrast, fixed GPUs are permanently installed units at specific airport locations, offering reliable and consistent power supply to parked aircraft. Both types are essential for maintaining airport efficiency and reducing aircraft emissions during ground operations.</p></p>
