<p><ul><li>Single Disc</li><li>Multi Disc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clutch packs market is divided into two main types: single disc and multi-disc. Single disc clutches are typically used in smaller vehicles and offer a simpler, more cost-effective solution for engagement and disengagement of the engine from the transmission. Multi-disc clutches, on the other hand, are used in high-performance and heavy-duty applications, providing greater torque capacity and smoother operation due to their larger surface area and multiple friction discs. This allows for enhanced performance in demanding conditions.</p></p>
