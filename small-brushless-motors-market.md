<p><strong>The Small Brushless Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outer Rotor Type</li><li>Inner Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small brushless motors market is primarily divided into two types: outer rotor and inner rotor motors. Outer rotor motors feature a rotor that encases the stator, providing higher torque and improved cooling efficiency, making them suitable for applications like drones and electric bikes. In contrast, inner rotor motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, resulting in a compact design with a higher power-to-weight ratio, ideal for robotics and consumer electronics. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
