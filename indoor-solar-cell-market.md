<p><p>The indoor solar cell market comprises various types, including amorphous silicon solar cells and photochemical solar cells. Amorphous silicon solar cells are made from non-crystalline silicon, offering flexibility and lightweight options suitable for low-light environments. In contrast, photochemical solar cells harness chemical reactions to convert light into energy, functioning efficiently in indoor settings with limited illumination. Both types cater to the growing demand for renewable energy solutions in residential and commercial indoor applications, promoting sustainability and energy efficiency.</p></p>
