<p><p>The RF test equipment market encompasses various essential tools used for analyzing and generating radio frequency signals. Oscilloscopes visualize voltage signals over time, enabling engineers to observe waveforms. Signal generators create specific frequency signals for testing devices. Spectrum analyzers measure the amplitude of signals across a frequency spectrum, allowing for interference detection and signal quality assessment. Network analyzers evaluate the performance of RF components by measuring parameters like reflection and transmission, crucial for optimizing communication systems.</p></p>
