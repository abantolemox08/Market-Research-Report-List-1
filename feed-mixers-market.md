Trailed feed mixers are towed by tractors, providing flexibility and ease of use on farms. Self-propelled feed mixers are independent machines that offer greater mobility and efficiency, ideal for larger operations. Stationary feed mixers remain fixed in one location, often used for larger batches and consistent feed production, catering to different farming needs and preferences.</p></p>
