<p><p>The Radio Frequency Coaxial Cable market includes two primary types based on impedance: 50Ω and 75Ω cables. The 50Ω cables are commonly used in applications requiring high power and low signal loss, such as radio communications and broadcasting. In contrast, 75Ω cables are typically utilized in video and broadcasting applications due to their lower attenuation and better performance at higher frequencies. Each type serves distinct purposes, meeting the requirements of various industries like telecommunications, broadcasting, and networking.</p></p>
