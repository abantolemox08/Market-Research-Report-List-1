<p><p>The Positive Photoresist Thinner market is categorized into two main types: non-concentrate and concentrate. Non-concentrate thinners are ready-to-use solutions that dilute photoresist materials without the need for additional mixing, offering convenience and efficiency. Conversely, concentrate thinners require dilution before use, providing flexibility to adjust viscosity according to specific application needs. Both types serve essential roles in the semiconductor industry, facilitating the clean removal of photoresist layers while ensuring optimal performance in lithography processes.</p></p>
