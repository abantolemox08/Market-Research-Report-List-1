<p><p>The Nickel Electroformed Molds market comprises two main processes: the Nickel Sulfamate Process and the Nickel Sulfate Electroforming Process. The Nickel Sulfamate Process uses a sulfamate nickel solution to produce high-quality, high-strength molds with excellent surface finish, ideal for complex shapes. In contrast, the Nickel Sulfate Electroforming Process utilizes a nickel sulfate solution, offering a simpler method but with varying mechanical properties and surface quality. Both processes cater to different industrial needs, including electronics and automotive applications.</p></p>
