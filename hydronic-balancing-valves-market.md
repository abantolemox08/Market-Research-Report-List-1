<p><strong>The Hydronic Balancing Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Balancing Valve</li><li>Dynamic Balancing Valve</li><li>PICV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydronic balancing valves market comprises three primary types: static balancing valves, dynamic balancing valves, and pressure independent control valves (PICVs). Static balancing valves are used for manual flow adjustment to ensure uniform distribution in hydronic systems. Dynamic balancing valves automatically adjust to varying pressures, maintaining consistent flow. PICVs combine features of both static and dynamic valves, allowing for automatic flow regulation without manual intervention. These valves enhance energy efficiency and comfort in heating and cooling applications by optimizing water flow.</p></p>
