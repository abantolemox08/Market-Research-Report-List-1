<p><p>The geomembrane liner market features several key types: </p><p>1. **HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene)**: Known for its strength and resistance to chemicals, it's widely used in landfill and wastewater applications.</p><p>2. **LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene)**: Offers flexibility and puncture resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring conformability.</p><p>3. **PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)**: Renowned for its durability and cost-effectiveness, used in various containment and waterproofing applications.</p><p>4. **FPP (Flexible Polypropylene)**: Features excellent chemical resistance and UV stability.</p><p>5. **Other**: Includes specialty liners likeEPDM and reinforced geomembranes, catering to unique project needs.</p></p>
