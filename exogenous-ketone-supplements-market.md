<p><p>The exogenous ketone supplements market consists of three main types: ketone salts, ketone esters, and MCT oil. Ketone salts combine ketones with minerals, providing a palatable source of energy and promoting ketosis. Ketone esters are pure forms of ketones that offer rapid absorption and energy spikes but may have a stronger taste. MCT oil, derived from coconut or palm oil, aids in ketone production in the liver and is favored for its versatility in cooking and supplementation. Each type serves distinct purposes in enhancing energy and supporting ketogenic lifestyles.</p></p>
