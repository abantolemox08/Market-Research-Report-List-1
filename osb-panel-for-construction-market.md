<p><p>OSB (Oriented Strand Board) panels are categorized into four main types based on their performance and use in construction. OSB/1 is suitable for interior non-load-bearing applications. OSB/2 is designed for load-bearing applications in dry conditions. OSB/3 is moisture-resistant and used for load-bearing in humid conditions. OSB/4 offers high load-bearing capacity and is suitable for use in heavy-duty applications, including external conditions. Each type addresses specific structural needs and environmental factors in construction.</p></p>
