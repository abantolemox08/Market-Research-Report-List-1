<p><p>Calcium aluminate cement (CAC) is categorized into various types based on alumina content, specifically CA40, CA50, CA60, CA70, and CA80, denoting the percentage of alumina present. CA40 contains 40% alumina and is suitable for general applications, while CA50 offers improved performance for structural uses. CA60 exhibits enhanced strength and durability, ideal for high-stress environments. CA70 and CA80 provide exceptional heat resistance and chemical stability, making them suitable for specialized applications such as refractory materials and corrosive environments.</p></p>
