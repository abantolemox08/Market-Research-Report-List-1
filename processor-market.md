<p><p>The processor market comprises several types based on the number of cores. Single-core processors, the oldest design, handle one task at a time, making them suitable for basic applications. Dual-core processors feature two cores, allowing for improved multitasking and better performance in moderate workloads. Quad-core processors, with four cores, provide even greater processing power, optimizing performance for demanding applications like gaming and video editing. As technology evolves, multi-core processors dominate the market, catering to the growing need for efficiency and speed.</p></p>
