<p><p>The intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) tools market comprises three main components: IVUS consoles, IVUS catheters, and accessories. IVUS consoles serve as the main processing units, offering imaging and diagnostic capabilities during procedures. IVUS catheters, inserted into blood vessels, are equipped with ultrasound transducers to capture detailed images of vascular structures. Accessories include guidewires, sheaths, and other supportive devices that enhance the functionality and effectiveness of IVUS systems. Together, these components play a crucial role in cardiovascular imaging and interventions.</p></p>
