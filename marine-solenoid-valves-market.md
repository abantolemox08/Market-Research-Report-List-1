<p><ul><li>Direct-acting Solenoid Valve</li><li>Sub-step Direct-acting Solenoid Valve</li><li>Pilot-type Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine solenoid valves market encompasses three主要类型: direct-acting solenoid valves, sub-step direct-acting solenoid valves, and pilot-type solenoid valves. Direct-acting valves operate directly on the solenoid's magnetic force, providing fast response times, while sub-step direct-acting valves offer finer control through multiple activation levels. Pilot-type solenoid valves utilize a smaller solenoid to control a larger valve, allowing for efficient operation at higher pressures and flow rates. Together, these valves play a crucial role in marine applications, ensuring reliable fluid control.</p></p>
