<p><p>The bulletproof jacket market comprises three primary types: soft vests, hard vests, and composite vests. Soft vests are lightweight and flexible, offering comfort and mobility, often designed for everyday use among law enforcement and security personnel. Hard vests provide enhanced protection through rigid armor plates, ideal for high-threat environments. Composite vests combine materials from both soft and hard types to balance weight and protection, catering to diverse operational needs. Each type serves specific applications based on threat levels and user requirements.</p></p>
