<p><strong>The Laboratory Autoclave Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Displacement Autoclave</li><li>Pre-vacuum Autoclave</li><li>Steam Flush Autoclave</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory autoclave market comprises various types tailored for different sterilization needs. Gravity displacement autoclaves utilize gravity to allow steam penetration, effective for sterilizing uncomplicated loads. Pre-vacuum autoclaves create a vacuum before steam introduction, enhancing sterilization for porous materials and dense loads. Steam flush autoclaves combine steam and vacuum cycles for thorough sterilization, particularly suitable for surgical instruments. Other market types may include mid-range and specialty autoclaves, designed for specific applications like waste sterilization or pharmaceutical research.</p></p>
