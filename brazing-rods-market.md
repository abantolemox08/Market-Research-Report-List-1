<p><p>Brazing rods are filler materials used in the brazing process, a metal-joining technique where a filler metal is melted and flowed into the joint between two or more metal parts. These rods are typically made from alloys such as aluminum-silicon, copper-phosphorus, silver, and nickel, depending on the application and the metals being joined. Brazing rods are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, HVAC, electronics, and construction due to their ability to create strong, leak-proof, and corrosion-resistant joints.</p><p>The Brazing Rods Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period. The Asia-Pacific region is emerging as a key market due to rapid industrialization and increasing manufacturing activities, further propelling the demand for brazing rods globally.</p></p>
