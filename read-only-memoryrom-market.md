<p><strong>The Read-Only Memory(ROM) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mask ROM</li><li>PROM</li><li>EPROM</li><li>EEPROM</li><li>Flash Memory</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Read-Only Memory (ROM) is a non-volatile storage technology essential for embedding firmware in devices. The market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Mask ROM** is permanently programmed during manufacturing and cannot be modified.</p><p>2. **PROM** can be programmed once after manufacturing.</p><p>3. **EPROM** is erasable with UV light, allowing multiple programming cycles.</p><p>4. **EEPROM** can be electrically erased and reprogrammed, providing flexibility.</p><p>5. **Flash Memory**, a modern advancement, enables rapid rewriting and is widely used in consumer electronics. Each type serves distinct applications based on reprogramming needs.</p></p>
