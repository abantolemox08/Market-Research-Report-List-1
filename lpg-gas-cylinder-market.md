<p><p>The LPG gas cylinder market comprises two primary types: metal LPG cylinders and composite LPG cylinders. Composite LPG cylinders, on the other hand, are made from lightweight materials like fiberglass, providing added portability and resistance to rust. The shift towards composite options is driven by consumer demand for safer, lighter, and more efficient gas storage solutions.</p></p>
