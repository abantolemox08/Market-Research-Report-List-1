<p><strong>The Industrial Exhaust Blowers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blower</li><li>Axial Blower</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial exhaust blowers market is primarily segmented into three types: centrifugal blowers, axial blowers, and others. Centrifugal blowers utilize centrifugal force to move air, offering high pressure and efficiency, making them suitable for various applications. Axial blowers generate airflow parallel to the axis of the fan, ideal for larger volumes of air at lower pressures. The "others" category encompasses specialized blowers like regenerative and positive displacement blowers, catering to specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
