<p><ul><li>Standalone Tracker</li><li>OBD Trackers</li><li>Advanced Trackers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS tracker market comprises three main types: Standalone Trackers, OBD Trackers, and Advanced Trackers. Standalone Trackers are portable devices that can be placed in vehicles or carried by individuals for tracking purposes. OBD Trackers connect directly to a vehicle's onboard diagnostics port, providing real-time data and tracking capabilities. Advanced Trackers offer additional features such as geofencing, driver behavior monitoring, and integration with mobile apps, catering to more complex tracking needs for both personal and fleet management applications.</p></p>
