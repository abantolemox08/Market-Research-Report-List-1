<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesive market is categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives use water as a primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, offering strong bonding capabilities but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Hot melt adhesives, which are thermoplastic materials, are applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, providing quick setting times and strong bonds, making them ideal for various applications in packaging and manufacturing.</p></p>
