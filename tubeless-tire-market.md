<p><p>The tubeless tire market comprises two primary types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric arranged radially, allowing for better flexibility, fuel efficiency, and improved handling, making them ideal for modern vehicles. In contrast, bias tires consist of crisscrossed layers, offering greater durability and resistance to punctures, which can be advantageous for heavy-duty applications. Together, these tire types cater to a diverse range of automotive needs, influencing performance, safety, and maintenance considerations in the market.</p></p>
