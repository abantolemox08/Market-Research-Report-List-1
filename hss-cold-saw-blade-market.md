<p><p>The HSS cold saw blade market primarily comprises M2 and M35 tool steel types. M2 tool steel is known for its excellent wear resistance and toughness, making it ideal for cutting a variety of materials. In contrast, M35 tool steel, which contains cobalt, offers enhanced heat resistance and improved cutting performance, particularly for harder materials. Both types cater to distinct industrial applications, with M35 being favored for precision and higher productivity tasks, while M2 is often used for general-purpose cutting.</p></p>
