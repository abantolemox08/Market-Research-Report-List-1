<p><p>The Flow-through Cell Dissolution Tester market includes two main types: UV On and UV Offline systems. </p><p>UV On systems measure drug dissolution in real-time using a built-in UV spectrophotometer, providing immediate analytical feedback during testing. </p><p>UV Offline systems, on the other hand, involve separate sampling and analysis steps, where the dissolution media is collected and analyzed later, offering flexibility in testing but a delay in results. Both types cater to varying regulatory and operational needs in pharmaceutical development.</p></p>
